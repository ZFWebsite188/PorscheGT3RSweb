Specifications of the Porsche 911 GT3RS

  • Engine: 4.0-liter, 6-cylinder, naturally aspirated
  • Horsepower: 518 hp @ 8,500 rpm
  • Torque: 469 Nm @ 6,300 rpm
  • Top Speed: 310 km/h (193 mph)
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): 3.2 seconds
  • Transmission: 7-speed PDK (Porsche Doppelkupplung)
  • Drive System: Rear-wheel drive
  • Weight: 1,450 kg (3,197 lbs)
  • Price: Starting at $187,500

The Porsche 911 GT3RS delivers exceptional performance and precision. It is a dream car for enthusiasts who value speed, style, and cutting-edge engineering.
